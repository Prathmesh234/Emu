## Google Vids ‚Äî Keyboard Shortcuts

Google Vids is a video creation app in Google Workspace for making presentations with video, audio, and scenes.

> Press `Ctrl + /` (Windows) or `‚åò + /` (macOS) to open the keyboard shortcuts help dialog.
> Press `Alt + /` (Windows) or `Option + /` (macOS) to open Tool finder (search the menus).
> Menu access keys: open any menu with keyboard, then type the underlined letter.

### Tips

- **`[` and `]`** activate trim handles on scenes ‚Äî use arrow keys to resize after activating.
- **Spacebar** toggles play/pause in the timeline.
- **`Ctrl + Shift + S`** / **`‚åò + Shift + S`** splits a scene at the playhead position (playhead must not be at the exact start/end).
- Use **`Ctrl + Alt + Shift + T`** / **`‚åò + Option + Shift + T`** and **`Ctrl + Alt + Shift + C`** / **`‚åò + Option + Shift + C`** to quickly jump between timeline and canvas.
- **`P`** selects the playhead when focus is already in the timeline.
- On macOS, `‚åò` replaces `Ctrl`, `Option` replaces `Alt`, and for Google Vids menus use `Ctrl + Option + letter`.
