## Google Vids — Keyboard Shortcuts (PC / Windows)

Google Vids is a video creation app in Google Workspace for making presentations with video, audio, and scenes.

> Press `Ctrl + /` to open the keyboard shortcuts help dialog.
> Press `Alt + /` to open Tool finder (search the menus).
> Menu access keys: open any menu with keyboard, then type the underlined letter.

### Tips

- **`[` and `]`** activate trim handles on scenes — use arrow keys to resize after activating.
- **Spacebar** toggles play/pause in the timeline.
- **`Ctrl + Shift + S`** splits a scene at the playhead position (playhead must not be at the exact start/end).
- Use **`Ctrl + Alt + Shift + T`** and **`Ctrl + Alt + Shift + C`** to quickly jump between timeline and canvas.
- **`P`** selects the playhead when focus is already in the timeline.
