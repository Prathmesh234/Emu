## Microsoft Teams ŌĆö Keyboard Shortcuts (macOS)

Microsoft Teams is a communication and collaboration app. On macOS it runs as a native desktop application. The left sidebar provides navigation between Activity, Chat, Teams, Calendar, Calls, and Files. The main area shows conversations, meeting content, or calendar views. A compose box appears at the bottom of chat/channel conversations.

> **Tip:** Press `Ōīś + .` (period) inside Teams to see the full keyboard shortcut list.

### Tips

- **Push to Talk:** Hold `Option + Spacebar` to temporarily unmute while in a meeting ŌĆö release to mute again.
- **Quick search:** `Ōīś + E` opens the universal search bar for finding messages, people, files, and channels.
- **Compose box:** Press `C` from any chat to jump straight to the compose box. Use `Ōīś + Shift + X` to expand it for rich formatting.
- **Navigation numbers:** `Ōīś + 1` through `Ōīś + 6` jump between the main sections in the left sidebar.
- When joining a meeting, the meeting toolbar appears at the top of the meeting window with controls for mute, video, share, reactions, and leave.
