## Gmail — Keyboard Shortcuts

Gmail is Google's email web app at mail.google.com. Most shortcuts require enabling first.

### How to Enable Keyboard Shortcuts

1. Open Gmail → click the **gear icon** (top-right) → **See all settings**
2. Under the **General** tab, scroll to **Keyboard shortcuts**
3. Select **Keyboard shortcuts on**
4. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom

> Press `?` (Shift + /) inside Gmail to see the full shortcut reference overlay.

### Tips

- Many Gmail shortcuts are **single-key** (no Ctrl needed) — e.g., `e` to archive, `r` to reply.
- Two-key sequences like `g` then `i` must be typed one after the other (not held simultaneously).
- `*` shortcuts for selection require pressing `*` (Shift + 8) first, then the letter.
- If shortcuts don't work, verify they are enabled in Settings.
- On Windows, `Ctrl` is used for modifier-key shortcuts. Single-key shortcuts are the same on both platforms.
